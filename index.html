What we do different

01

Competition as validation

We use competitions as a forcing function for reliability under constraints: limited time, limited budget, strict rules, real field conditions.

02

Hardware-first autonomy

We don’t treat autonomy as “just software” We integrate all: sensing, compute, power, EMI, mechanical integration, logging.

03

Accessible & Fast iteration

We use modular FDM printed components to iterate the design rapidly without adopting existing limitations or investing time into a composite frame.
