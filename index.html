What makes this team unique

01

Competition-grade validation

We use competitions as a forcing function for reliability under constraints: limited time, limited budget, strict rules, real field conditions.

02

Hardware-first autonomy

We don’t treat autonomy as “just software.” We build the stack as a system: sensing, compute, power integrity, EMI, mechanical integration, logging, and testability.

03

Fast iteration, real engineering

Short cycles, tight scope, measurable milestones. We design for the build, not just for the render.

FAQ

Are you offering engineering services?

No. We’re a research-focused student team seeking sponsorship to fund development and demonstrations.

What will sponsorship be used for?

Manufacturing iterations, compute + sensors, airframe prototyping, test equipment, and competition testing/travel.

What do sponsors receive?

Visibility, recruiting access, progress updates, and demo opportunities. Sponsorship does not imply ownership of IP unless explicitly agreed in writing.

Can we sponsor with parts instead of cash?

Yes. We accept in-kind sponsorships (PCB fab/assembly credit, sensors, compute, RF gear, batteries, tooling).

How do we start?

Request the sponsor deck and we’ll propose the best tier based on what you can provide.
